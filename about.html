Our Story

DashDose was started in 2024 after our founder spent 2 hours walking around Eastlands at night looking for an open pharmacy for his sick daughter. That night, we realized there was no simple way to know which pharmacies were open and which had the medicine you need.

We built DashDose to solve this problem. Today, we help hundreds of Kenyans find open pharmacies and available medicine every day.
