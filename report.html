Why Reporting Matters

DashDose depends on people like you to stay accurate. We do not have someone checking every pharmacy every day. If a pharmacy has wrong hours, a disconnected number, or medicine listed as available when it is not - someone is going to waste their time walking there for nothing.

It takes less than a minute to report it. And the next person who checks DashDose will get the right information because of you.

How We Handle Reports

When you submit a report, our team checks it within 48 hours. If we can verify the information, we update it straight away. For things we cannot verify remotely - like overcharging - we flag the pharmacy and follow up directly. We do not remove pharmacies based on one report alone. We check first.
